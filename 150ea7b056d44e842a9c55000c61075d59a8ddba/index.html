A fusion of contemporary living and the finest nautical heritage , PANTHALASSA reflects an outstanding creative synthesis between perini Navi and Foster & Partners. The original design brief called for a seamless relationship between the exterior and interior, this has been surpassed. Natural daylight suffuses PANTHALASSA's interior through the unique skylights that channel light from the sundeck and oversized portholes. The clean lines and uncluttered style of her interior offer contemporary living at its best. Central to the yacht , the gracefully flowing stairwell is wrapped in a translucent acrylic enclosure allowing light to flow through the main saloon. A tactile and warm abience is created through the use of warm, natural organic tones and the combination of teak flooring with saddle leather wall panelling and granite surfaces. Flexible multifunctional spaces offer a host of well conceived opportunities for social intreraction or intimate gatherings. the dining table can become a coffee table , the library is positioned with views over open water for a feeling of deep relaxtion and enhancing contemplation. The boardroom with its video conferencing facilities is also a perfect dining area and the sweeping sofa in the saloon becomes a bar. Electric glazed screens allow the entire main saloon , dining area and wheelhouse to be divided or unified into a one large living space. Her communications are state of the art as are her in house entretainment systems. For those who want endless distraction on the water her list of toys is unsurpassed.
